Fibonacci sequence

The Fibonacci sequence is defined through the recurrence relation F_n =
F_(n-1) + F_(n-2). It can also be expressed in closed form:

F_n = round(1 / sqrt(5) phi^n), phi = (1 + sqrt(5)) / 2

The first 8 numbers of the sequence are:

  F_1   F_2   F_3   F_4   F_5   F_6   F_7   F_8
  ----- ----- ----- ----- ----- ----- ----- -----
  1     1     2     3     5     8     13    21
